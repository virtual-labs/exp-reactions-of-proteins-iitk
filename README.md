### About the Experiment 

In this experiment investigation of the effects of temperature on the physical properties and denaturation of proteins will be done which is required to observe the changes in opacity, consistency, and protein denaturation at various temperatures and to demonstrate the heat-induced structural changes in proteins, including coagulation and precipitation
